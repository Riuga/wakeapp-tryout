What is Wakeapp?+

Wakeapp is the game-making studio known for tabletop-game realisations like Poker, Durak, Solitaire, Aboba, and more! We found out a way to play favourite games with your friends online.

You may heard of us from an articles, streams, social-media or commercials. Maybe you heard about as from your friends. Eather way you're here and we are happy to see you!

What is Game Pack?+

We create a variety of games designed to entertain everyone in the room via Zoom calls or Twitch streams. Designing games, writing games, mini-games, stealth games... no partygoer is left behind.

Every year since 2014 we release a new collection of games packaged as the Jackbox Party Pack. In each package you will find 5 different games that can accommodate 1-8 players. If you have a lot of people in your group, then we're happy to be popular and most of our games get the crowd playing, meaning they can keep jumping and having fun!

We also have special horror games like Aboba that are available outside of party packs.

How to start a game?+

You purchased a package. You've gathered your team. All you need is to start the game and play! Here we have helpful video tutorials and step-by-step instructions on how to get all your players hooked into the game. This page also includes information on how to access additional channels and options for each game.

The important thing is to ensure that everyone in your party (in person or remotely) can see and hear the game screen. This will allow them to access room codes, work together, see what's going on in the game, and play together.

What if I have issues with game?+

We have a great support site with lots of troubleshooting tips and tricks here. You can also donate a ticket to our support team. They try to respond within 1-3 business hours. Holidays may take a little longer if we're affected.

However, our strong recommendation is that if a player's mobile device disconnects during gameplay... they should try updating their browser. Most of the time they meet right away. If not, please let us know. As we say: no partygoer is left behind.
